## Is Pampered Chef a Pyramid Scheme or MLM?

**No, Pampered Chef is not a pyramid scheme**—it's a legitimate multi-level marketing (MLM) company that's been in business for over 40 years.

Here's the difference:

### Legitimate MLM (Pampered Chef):
- ✅ Sells real, high-quality products that people actually want
- ✅ Consultants earn money primarily from product sales, not recruiting
- ✅ No requirement to maintain large inventory
- ✅ Owned by Berkshire Hathaway (Warren Buffett's company) since 2002
- ✅ Strong product warranties and customer satisfaction guarantees

### Pyramid Schemes (Illegal):
- ❌ Focus on recruiting over product sales
- ❌ Require large upfront inventory purchases
- ❌ Promise unrealistic income with minimal work
- ❌ Products are overpriced or low-quality

**Bottom line**: Pampered Chef is a established company with quality products. While becoming a consultant requires an initial investment, most people buy Pampered Chef because they genuinely love the kitchen tools—not because of a business opportunity.
