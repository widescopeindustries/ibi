## Is Avon a Pyramid Scheme or MLM?

**No, Avon is not a pyramid scheme**—it's a legitimate multi-level marketing (MLM) company and one of the oldest direct sales companies in the world (founded 1886).

Here's the difference:

### Legitimate MLM (Avon):
- ✅ Sells real, quality beauty products trusted for over 135 years
- ✅ Representatives earn money primarily from product sales
- ✅ No requirement to maintain inventory (order as you sell)
- ✅ Strong satisfaction guarantee on products
- ✅ Recognized as a Fortune 500 company

### Pyramid Schemes (Illegal):
- ❌ Focus on recruiting over product sales
- ❌ Require large upfront inventory purchases
- ❌ Promise unrealistic income with minimal work
- ❌ Products are overpriced or low-quality

**Bottom line**: Avon is an established beauty brand with millions of satisfied customers worldwide. Most people buy Avon because they love the affordable prices and quality products.
