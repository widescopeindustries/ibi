## Is Mary Kay a Pyramid Scheme or MLM?

**No, Mary Kay is not a pyramid scheme**—it's a legitimate multi-level marketing (MLM) company with over 60 years in business and millions of satisfied customers worldwide.

Here's the difference:

### Legitimate MLM (Mary Kay):
- ✅ Sells real, high-quality beauty products backed by clinical testing
- ✅ Consultants earn money primarily from product sales to customers
- ✅ No requirement to maintain large inventory
- ✅ Strong satisfaction guarantee on all products
- ✅ Recognized as one of the best companies for women entrepreneurs

### Pyramid Schemes (Illegal):
- ❌ Focus on recruiting over product sales  
- ❌ Require large upfront inventory purchases
- ❌ Promise unrealistic income with minimal work
- ❌ Products are overpriced or low-quality

**Bottom line**: Mary Kay is a established beauty brand with a loyal customer base. Most people buy Mary Kay because they love the skincare results and personalized service—not because of a business opportunity.
