## Is Scentsy a Pyramid Scheme or MLM?

**No, Scentsy is not a pyramid scheme**—it's a legitimate multi-level marketing (MLM) company that's been in business for over 20 years.

Here's the difference:

### Legitimate MLM (Scentsy):
- ✅ Sells real, high-quality products that customers love
- ✅ Consultants earn money primarily from product sales
- ✅ No requirement to maintain inventory
- ✅ Strong 90-day satisfaction guarantee
- ✅ Lifetime warranty on warmers

### Pyramid Schemes (Illegal):
- ❌ Focus on recruiting over product sales
- ❌ Require large upfront inventory purchases
- ❌ Promise unrealistic income with minimal work
- ❌ Products are overpriced or low-quality

**Bottom line**: Scentsy is a established company with loyal customers. Most people buy Scentsy because they genuinely love the flameless warmers and signature scents—not because of a business opportunity.
